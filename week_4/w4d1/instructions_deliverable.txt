### 6D) Primary Deliverable — Mini Lookup Console

Build a script that:

1. Starts with a `prices` dict (at least 5 SKUs, like `"sku1": 9.99`).
2. Runs a `while True` loop asking for an action: `lookup`, `set`, or `quit`.
3. `lookup`: ask for SKU, print price or `"Unknown sku"` (no crash).
4. `set`: ask for SKU and price, convert to `float` with `try/except`, add/update the dict.
5. `quit`: break the loop.
6. Unknown actions get a help message.

**Must use:** dict literal or `{}`, `dict[key] = value`, `in` or `.get()`, at least one f-string.

**Constraint:** No list comprehensions or dict comprehensions. No functions (Week 5). No `.keys()`/`.values()`/`.items()` (tomorrow).

**What could go wrong?** What happens if the user enters a price like `"abc"`? Does your script handle it?
